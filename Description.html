Description


diagram of fire extinguisher with notes

This is a diagram of the fire extinguisher pictured in the photos above this one. A fire extinguisher is used in order to either mitigate or extinguish a fire. It is used in emergency situations, the emotional and mental state of users during this time is an important factor to consider when designing such an interface. One of the key components of this interface are the two handles. One handle is on top and the other below to create a clamp like ‘trigger’. There is a pin that is meant to be pulled out before use. There is also a meter that gages the PSI of the fire extinguisher.
